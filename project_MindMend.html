DESCRIPTION

A mobile app created in Figma designed as an accessible and personalized way to improve their mental health. The app helps users regulate thier emotion and cultivate mindful habits that improve their mental state. Users can reflect thier thoughts in a mood jornal, complete daily mindful task, participate in breathing and meditation excersises and listen to music, podcast and sound for any emotion. The app also provide personalized guides and activities to do or listen and even chat with a AI mindfullness mentor to help answers your mental health querie. Figma link here
