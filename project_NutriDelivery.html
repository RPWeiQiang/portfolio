DESCRIPTION

A React Travel List App is a user-friendly tool designed to help travelers organize and manage their packing list efficiently. Users can add items along with their quantities, mark items as important, and check them off when packed. The app allows individual item deletion, clearing the entire list, or removing only packed items. A search function helps users quickly find specific items. Additionally, the app tracks packing progress by displaying the percentage of items packed, ensuring users stay on top of their preparation. This intuitive app simplifies travel planning and ensures nothing is forgotten.
