CATERGORY :

  • UI/UX design

CREATED WITH :

DESCRIPTION

A mobile app created in Figma designed for busy individuals who want to maintain a healthy lifestyle without the hassle. The app delivers fresh, nutritious meals tailored to your dietary preferences and health goals. Each meal comes with detailed nutritional information, including calorie count, allowing users to make informed choices. With built-in tracking features, users can log their daily food intake, monitor their nutrition and calorie consumption, and stay on top of their health goals. Figma link here
